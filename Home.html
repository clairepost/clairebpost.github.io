Welcome!

Hello! This is my website to house my academic projects, papers, and writing interests amongst other topics. 

    Claire         Ben√©t   Post     

I am currently a Senior at the University of Southern California pursuing a BS in Computational Linguistics and a BA in International Relations. I am set to graduate in May of 2023. My primary passion is using linguistics to discover ways to help proliferate the use of endangered languages. I would describe myself as a nerd who can spend endless hours teaching myself phonetic transcriptions, reading up on machine learning techniques, and emailing my academic friends with paper ideas. Right now, I am truly enjoying my love of language learning as I study abroad in Santiago, Chile at the University of Chile and the Pontifica Catolica University here for the semester.

Coming from rural Arkansas, finding a passion for linguistics USC has given me a totally new life. In the future, I see myself pursuing a PhD in a computational linguistics field. I dream of working on translation technology for endangered languages.

On the day to day, I love to rock climb, mountaineer, ice climb, and come up with yoga flows. Going to an arts high school in Arkansas, I have developed a deep passion for poetry and can often times be seen jotting down my thoughts on the notes app of my phone.
